How to Apply for a New Aadhaar Card?

  • Eligibility : Available for all Indian residents, including newborns.
  • Required docs : Proof of Identity, Address & DOB.
  • Requirements : Biometric data (photo, fingerprints, eye scan) required.
  • Aadhaar is free & takes 7-90 days to process.

How to Apply for a Pan Card?

  • Eligibility : Check eligibility & gather required documents.
  • Apply via NSDL or UTIITSL and Complete Form 49A and upload documents.
  • Track Application Status
  • PAN card is issued within 7-15 days.
